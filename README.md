## Overview
This repository contains my Digital Literacy case study report.  
The report analyzes how students use digital tools and the difference between
digital participation and true digital literacy.

The case study focuses on the experiences of a student named Arjun and explores
important topics related to responsible digital behavior.

## Topics Covered
Digital identity and ethics
Online collaboration and responsibility
Cyber awareness and phishing risks
Communication and digital etiquette
Reflection on digital literacy
